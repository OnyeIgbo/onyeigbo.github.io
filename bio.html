Onye, can be considered as an "Tech enthusiast", she enjoys all things technology from a range of devices such as tablets, mobile phones - all things technology Onye is the one to go to!

I beleive that was the beginning of something that was ultimately the start of enthusiam of wanting to be creative. Earlier as a child Onye's mother would call her the 'engineer' of the household as she was the go-to whenever there was an issue from; tablets, devices to even turning on the TV!

Since then Onye grew this obsession of technology, always up to date with the latest gadgets and wanting to hopefully one day create something as the great Steve Jobs.

Overall, I belive that Onye was brought into this world to be a creative, inovative , and a problem solver.
